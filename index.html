About Crispy's

Since 1985, we've been serving up the most delicious fried chicken in town. Our secret recipe has been passed down through generations, ensuring every bite is packed with flavor and crispy perfection.

We use only the freshest, locally sourced ingredients and our chickens are hand-breaded daily to guarantee the highest quality meal for our customers.
